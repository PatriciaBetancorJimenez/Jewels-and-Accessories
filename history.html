Meet the family

Our slogan has always been "Dream big, work hard"



Winden 1885

Robert Bösendorfer

He was the head of family and founder of the business in 1884 in the town of Winden, Germany

Martin Bösendorfer

Martin is Robert's eldest son and started to lead the business after Robert died.

The factory

The building is located on the outskirts of Winden and has provided the town with employment and economical growth for centuries.

The mine

Our diamonds and work materials such as: gold, silver, etc. come from a very remote place in Siberia. They get transported to the factory by trains and get treated and purified in Winden. The journey from Russia can take 8 days.

The miners

They are the heart and soul of our business. Thanks to their hard work, we are able to sell high quality gemstones to our clients. We currently have about 200 miners employed.
